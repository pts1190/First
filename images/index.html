"Integrating Wellness Into Your Occupational Health and Safety Program"

Dr. Roman Kownacki

March 14, 2008
Hs Lordships, Berkeley, CA

Dr. Kownacki has been with Kaiser Permanente since 1999 and specializes in Occupational Medicine and Preventive Medicine.

Dr. Kownacki will discuss the importance of wellness programs to health and safety programs. Healthy employees are less likely to be injured and have greater productivity. Dr. Kownack will discuss the basics of incorporating wellness into health and safety programs, managing wellness programs for optimum results and the return on investment of wellness programs.

The luncheon seminar will be held at Hs Lordships, 199 Seawall Drive, in the Berkeley Marina, (510)843-2733 (map). Registration starts at 11:15 am and the presentation follows at noon.
